The dataset employed is the Synapse multi-organ CT dataset from the MICCAI 2015 Multi-Atlas Abdomen Labeling Challenge. The Synapse multi-organ CT dataset comes from https://www.synapse.org/#!Synapse:syn3193805/wiki/217789, Example from: https://doi.org/10.7303/syn3193805. It includes 3,779 clinical CT images of the abdomen. For training purposes, 18 samples (2,211 axial slices) were randomly selected, leaving the remaining 12 samples for testing.
